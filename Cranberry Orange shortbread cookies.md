- [ ] 1 cup unsalted butter at room temperature
- [ ] 1/2 cup granulated sugar
- [ ] zest of one orange or tangerine (just the orange part, not the bitter white pith)
- [ ] 1 heaping cup fresh cranberries chopped up
- [ ] 2 cups [[Standard Gluten Free Flour|GF Flour]]
- [ ] 1 tsp pure vanilla extract

Steps:
1. Cream the butter, sugar, and zest together 
2. Add flour and vanilla 
3. Add chopped/minced cranberries 
4. Form dough into log and chill in fridge for 3hrs or overnight 
5. Preheat 350F
6. Slice the dough into 1/3in slices and place on the cookie sheet, two inches apart
7. Bake for 10-12mins
8. Rest on cookie sheet for 5-10mins
9. Then rest on cooling racks for 10-15mins
